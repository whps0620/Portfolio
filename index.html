MSc Thesis · Wageningen University · Feb 2025 碩士論文 · 瓦赫寧恩研究大學 · 2025年2月
Mapping Gas Permeability of Sustainable Packaging via Clustering Algorithms 以分群演算法映射永續包裝材料氣體滲透性
Food Quality Design · Supervisor: Deniz Turan Kunter 食品品質設計(Food Quality Design) · 指導教師：Deniz Turan Kunter
📄 Published · npj Science of Food (Nature Portfolio, 2026)已發表 · npj Science of Food（Nature 旗下期刊，2026）  ↗
Background研究背景

Plastic packaging waste costs the global economy $80–120 billion per year, yet ~95% of plastic packaging value is lost after a single use. While sustainable alternatives (reusable, recyclable, biodegradable) are rapidly emerging — research publications on sustainable packaging surged from 70 (2015) to 1,338 (2024) on Web of Science — they often lack the gas barrier performance required to preserve food quality. Packaging materials gas barrier mainly focuses on oxygen transmission rate (OTR) and water vapor transmission rate (WVTR). Different foods have vastly different barrier needs: instant coffee requires ultra-low OTR & WVTR to prevent oxidation, fresh produce needs higher gas permeability to allow respiration, and cheese demands low OTR but moderate WVTR. Despite this diversity, no systematic data-driven tool existed to map sustainable material properties to these food-specific needs.

塑膠包裝廢棄物每年為全球經濟造成 800–1,200 億美元損失，約 95% 的包裝塑膠在一次使用後即喪失價值。隨著永續替代材料（可重複使用、可回收、可生物降解）快速興起——Web of Science 相關論文從 2015 年 70 篇快速增加至 2024 年 1,338 篇——這些材料的氣體阻隔性能往往仍不符合食品保鮮要求。包裝材料的阻隔性主要關注於氧氣(Oxygen transmission rate, OTR) 與水氣(Water vapor transmission rate, WVTR) 的通透性不同食品對阻隔性的需求差異懸殊：即溶咖啡需要極低 OTR & WVTR 防止氧化，新鮮農產品需要較高氣體滲透性以利呼吸，起司則需要低 OTR 但中等 WVTR。儘管如此，目前仍缺乏系統化資料驅動工具，能將永續材料的滲透性能與食品保鮮需求精準匹配。

Objective研究目標

This thesis addressed two core research questions: (1) Which clustering algorithms most accurately classify sustainable packaging materials based on their gas permeability? (2) Which sustainable materials are best suited for packaging different food categories based on barrier needs?

本論文聚焦兩個核心研究問題：（1）哪種分群演算法能根據氣體滲透性，最精確地分類永續包裝材料？（2） 根據氣體滲透性，哪些永續材料最適合用作各類食品的包裝？研究的創新點在於結合 NLP 文獻資料擷取與非監督式機器學習，銜接材料科學與食品技術之間的鴻溝，並將研究成果部署為即時互動工具。

Approach研究方法

Built on a text-mined dataset of 1,591 entries from 49 scientific articles (2000–2016). Of these, 22 articles yielded 197 gas permeability entries via automated NLP extraction; the remaining 27 articles contained additional permeability data not captured automatically, so OTR, WVTR, temperature, and RH were extracted manually — expanding the gas permeability dataset to 295 entries in total. Applied SpaCy 3.7.8 NLP with dependency parsing to automatically identify material names, modification types, and secondary components from paper abstracts and tables. The ETL pipeline standardised 34 unique measurement units to a common basis (OTR at 23°C/50%RH; WVTR at 25°C/50%RH), performed deduplication, and applied IQR-based outlier detection. Features were log₁₀-transformed to handle the wide span of gas permeability values across material categories prior to clustering.

透過 Web of Science 篩選 1,591 筆文獻資料，最終提取 295 筆氣體滲透性數據（22 篇文章自動 NLP 解析 197 筆 + 手動擷取 98 筆）。運用 SpaCy 3.7.8 NLP 依存句法分析，從論文摘要與表格中自動識別材料名稱、改質類型與次級成分。ETL 將 34 種不同計量單位標準化至統一基準（OTR：23°C/50%RH；WVTR：25°C/50%RH），並執行去重複與 IQR 異常值偵測。為處理不同材料類別間氣體滲透性數值的大幅跨度，分群前對特徵進行 log₁₀ 轉換。

Model Structure模型架構

Benchmarked three clustering algorithms — K-Means, GMM & DBSCAN — on log-transformed OTR & WVTR features. DBSCAN hyperparameters were tuned to ε = 1.17, MinPts = 4 via k-distance graph analysis. Model performance evaluated using both Silhouette Score (compactness & separation) and Davies-Bouldin Index (intra- vs inter-cluster distance). Best-performing clusters deployed as an interactive Streamlit dashboard enabling researchers and packaging engineers to query material clusters against specific food barrier needs.

對 log 轉換後的 OTR & WVTR 特徵，比較三種分群演算法（K-Means、GMM、DBSCAN）。DBSCAN 超參數透過 k-距離圖分析調整至 ε = 1.17、MinPts = 4。以 Silhouette Score （緊湊性與分離度）及 Davies-Bouldin 指標（群內 vs 群間距離）評估模型表現。最佳分群結果部署為互動式 Streamlit 儀表板，供研究人員與包裝工程師依食品保鮮需求查詢對應材料群組。

PythonSpaCy NLP DBSCANK-Means GMMScikit-learn PandasStreamlitETL

DBSCAN achieved the best clustering — Silhouette Score 0.910, Davies-Bouldin Index 0.374 — outperforming K-Means and GMM. Key findings: nanocomposite materials (e.g. nano-clay, nano-SiO₂ coatings) delivered the lowest OTR & WVTR, making them ideal candidates for high-barrier foods such as instant coffee and processed meats. Conventional biopolymers (PLA, starch) fell into mid-barrier clusters suited for fresh produce. Several bio-based films were identified as unsuitable for moisture-sensitive products due to high WVTR. The Streamlit dashboard allows users to input a target food product and retrieve ranked packaging material clusters in real time.

DBSCAN 分群效果最佳 — Silhouette Score 0.910，Davies-Bouldin Index 0.374，優於 K-Means 與 GMM。

主要發現：奈米複合材料（如奈米黏土、奈米 SiO₂ 塗層）具有最低 OTR & WVTR，最適合高阻隔性食品（如即溶咖啡、加工肉品）。傳統生物聚合物（PLA、澱粉）落入中等阻隔群組，適用於新鮮農產品。部分生物基薄膜因 WVTR 偏高，不適用於對水分敏感的產品。Streamlit 儀表板允許使用者輸入目標食品，即時獲取排名前列的包裝材料群組建議。

Predictive Model for Void Fraction in Pasty Bouillon Cubes 雞湯塊乾混料空隙率預測模型
Foods Innovation Centre · Dept: Cooking Aids & Mini Meals 食品創新中心 · 部門：烹飪輔助 & 迷你餐點
Background研究背景

A global FMCG company's bouillon cube production relied on trial-and-error for fat content — risking overuse of costly fat ingredients (~$1,070/ton). Particle size distribution (PSD) and particle shape govern the void fraction, which directly influence fat requirements.

全球 FMCG 企業的雞湯塊生產依靠試錯法確定脂肪含量，導致原料可能過量使用。原料的粒徑分佈（Particle Size Distribution, PSD）與形狀決定空隙率，進而預估脂肪用量。

Objective研究目標

Build a data-driven framework to predict void fraction from PSD and particle shape, enabling targeted fat/filler adjustments for optimized recipe design and reduced production costs.

建立資料驅動框架，從 PSD 與原料形狀預測空隙率，得以脂肪與填充物精準調整，優化配方設計並降低生產成本。

Approach研究方法

Characterized 130 ingredients using a laser diffractometer. Measured void fraction via a centrifugation-based oil displacement method. Conducted systematic binary & ternary mixture experiments to uncover particle packing mechanisms (filling vs. inclusion effects).

使用雷射粒徑儀量測 130 種原料，以離心油置換法量測空隙率，並進行系統性二元與三元混合實驗，揭示粒子堆積機制（填充效應與包覆效應）。

Particle size distribution (PSD) of 9 representative ingredient clusters (A–I, logarithmic scale) 9 個代表性原料群集（A–I）之粒徑分佈曲線（對數尺度）

Model Structure模型架構

Developed a PSD decision tree (9 clusters A–I) classifying ingredients by particle size, span, shape & cohesiveness. Designed a DoE (100 trials, JMP 17) to systematically investigate multi-ingredient void fraction. Fitted a Standard Least Squares (SLS) model linking PSD cluster compositions to void fraction output.

建立 粒徑分佈決策樹（9 群集 A–I），依粒徑、展距、形狀與黏聚性分類原料；設計 DoE（100 組試驗，JMP 17）系統性探討多組分空隙率；建立 SLS 預測模型，將粒徑分佈群集組成連結至空隙率輸出。

PythonDoE JMP 17PCA SLS ModelDecision Tree

Fine particles (<1/7 salt size) identified as key void fillers. SLS model reached R²=0.99, cutting R&D trial costs.

確認細顆粒（<鹽粒 1/7）為空隙率降低關鍵填充物。SLS 模型達 R²=0.99。
